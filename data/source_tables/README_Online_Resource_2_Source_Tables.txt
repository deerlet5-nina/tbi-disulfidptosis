Article title: Transcriptomic Prioritization of a Disulfidptosis-Like Transporter-Actin Stress Axis After Traumatic Brain Injury
Journal: Neurochemical Research
Authors: Chenxu Zhang and Mingyang Zhang
Corresponding author: mingyangzhang@suda.edu.cn

This archive contains comma-separated source tables used by the optimized public transcriptomics manuscript. The tables are derived from public GEO datasets and the processed analyses supplied with the original submission package. Table_S10 adds a processed-evidence robustness audit that reweights existing evidence layers only; it does not add wet-lab data and does not prove disulfidptotic cell death.
