Article title: Transcriptomic Prioritization of a Disulfidptosis-Like Transporter-Actin Stress Axis After Traumatic Brain Injury
Journal: Neurochemical Research
Authors: Chenxu Zhang and Mingyang Zhang
Corresponding author: mingyangzhang@suda.edu.cn

This archive contains the analysis and manuscript-construction scripts supplied with the original public transcriptomics package plus the added dry-evidence robustness audit script.
